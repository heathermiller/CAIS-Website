Workshops & Tutorials

ACM Conference on Agentic and AI Systems (CAIS 2026)

San Francisco • May 26, 2026

Overview

CAIS 2026 will host workshops and tutorials on Tuesday, May 26, 2026, the day before the main conference begins. These sessions provide deep dives into practical methods, frameworks, and emerging research directions in compound AI systems.

We invite proposals for half-day or full-day workshops that bring together researchers and practitioners to explore focused topics within the CAIS scope.

Topics of Interest

We welcome workshop proposals on topics including but not limited to:

  • DSPy and programmatic prompt optimization
  • Multi-agent system architectures
  • RAG systems and retrieval optimization
  • Tool-augmented language models
  • Evaluation frameworks for compound AI
  • MLOps for compound AI systems
  • Safety and alignment in agentic systems
  • Debugging and observability tools
  • Production deployment case studies
  • Emerging research directions

Proposal Guidelines

Workshop proposals should include:

  • Title and abstract: Clear description of the workshop focus
  • Organizers: Names, affiliations, and brief bios of organizers
  • Format: Half-day or full-day, with tentative schedule
  • Audience: Expected attendance and target participants
  • History: Previous editions (if applicable) with attendance numbers

Detailed submission instructions will be posted when the application opens on February 9, 2026.
