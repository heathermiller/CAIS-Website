Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: December 2025

CAIS 2026 (the "Conference") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at caisconf.org (the "Site").

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

Association for Computing Machinery (ACM)
1601 Broadway, 10th Floor
New York, NY 10019-7434
United States

For privacy inquiries related to this conference, contact: contact@caisconf.org
For ACM privacy matters: privacy@acm.org

Information We Collect

Analytics Data (with your consent)

When you accept cookies, we use Google Analytics 4 to collect:

  • Pages you visit and how long you spend on them
  • How you arrived at our site (referral source)
  • General geographic location (country/city level, not precise)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Interactions with site features

This data is aggregated and anonymized. We cannot identify you personally from analytics data.

Information You Provide

If you contact us via email or submit information through registration (when available), we collect the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name, email address, and affiliation.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve the website: Understand how visitors use the site to make it more useful
  • Plan the conference: Gauge interest and optimize content
  • Respond to inquiries: Reply to questions or requests you send us
  • Send updates: If you opt in, provide conference announcements

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis for Processing

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and similar laws, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

Analytics Data

Legal basis: Consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a))

We only collect analytics data after you explicitly accept cookies via our consent banner.

Contact Inquiries

Legal basis: Legitimate Interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

Processing your inquiries is necessary for our legitimate interest in responding to your questions and requests.

Server Logs

Legal basis: Legitimate Interest (GDPR Article 6(1)(f))

Our hosting provider collects logs necessary for security, performance monitoring, and abuse prevention.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality.

  • cais_cookie_consent — Stores your cookie preference (1 year)

Analytics Cookies (Optional)

Set only if you accept. Provided by Google Analytics.

  • _ga — Distinguishes users (2 years)
  • _ga_* — Maintains session state (2 years)

Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit our site, you'll see a consent banner. You can:

  • Accept: Analytics cookies will be set
  • Decline: No analytics cookies will be set; we won't track your visit

To change your choice later, click the Cookie Preferences link in the page footer. This will reset your choice and display the consent banner again.

Third-Party Services

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4, provided by Google LLC, to analyze site traffic. If you consent to analytics cookies, data is processed by Google under their privacy policy. Google may transfer data to servers in the United States under Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission.

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy | Google's Data Transfer Framework | Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Hosting

This site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs (IP address, browser type, pages requested) for security and performance purposes, processed under their legitimate interest in maintaining service security. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.

Content Delivery Networks

This site uses jsDelivr CDN to deliver CSS and font files (Bootstrap framework). When you load pages, your browser connects to jsDelivr servers, which may receive your IP address and browser information. jsDelivr operates servers globally. See jsDelivr's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access: Request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing activities
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time via the Cookie Preferences link
  • Lodge a complaint: If you believe your data protection rights have been violated, you have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in the EU Member State of your habitual residence, place of work, or place of the alleged infringement

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@caisconf.org.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained in Google Analytics for 14 months, after which it is automatically deleted. Email correspondence is retained as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for our records.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes may be communicated via a site banner.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:

Email: contact@caisconf.org
